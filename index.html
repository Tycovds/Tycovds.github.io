About me

I am Tyco van der Steen. A (junior) front-end developer based in Amsterdam. The first time I got in touch with web development was after high school and I immediately fell in love with it. My thirst for knowledge goes hand in hand with the never-ending information about the web and new technologies.

Besides development, I enjoy fishing, hiking and gaming. During the day, I like going outside with my dog to get some fresh air and when the sun goes down, it is time to go online and chill with friends.

If I were to describe my personality, I would say I can be quiet and serious, because I want to efficiently finish a task, but when it is time to relax, I am pretty goofy.
